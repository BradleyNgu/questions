Let's break this down into two cases.

1. Determine A

   A = divisible by 4

   $A = \{ 4,8,12,...,96,100 \}$

   $ |A| = 25 $

2. Determine B

   B = divisible by 5

   $B = \{ 5,10,15,...,95,100 \}$

   $ |B| = 20 $

3. Determine the Intersection

   $ |A \cap B| = 5 $

4. Profit

   $ |A \cup B| = |A| + |B| - |A \cap B| $

   $ |A \cup B| = 25 + 20 - 5 $

   $ |A \cup B| = 40 $

   Pr($A \cup B$) = $ \frac{40}{100} $

   Pr($A \cup B$) = $ \frac{2}{5} $
